-   Opcode: **0x3F**
-   Short name: **HMPMAX3**
-   Long name: HP/MP Maximum

#### Memory layout

| 0x3F |
|------|

#### Arguments

None.

#### Description

Restores full HP and MP of every party member, but not those outside the
current party. It does not remove status effects such as Fury or
Sadness, but will revive KOd party members.
